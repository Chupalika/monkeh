Note that Discord has a character limit. More than 5 scores usually exceeds the limit.\n__**Examples**__\n``{cp}score b=898597`` - your high scores for beatmap 898597\n``{cp}score b=898597{pd}u=Chupalika{pd}mods=DT`` - Chupalika's DT score on beatmap 898597\n``{cp}score b=495651{pd}5-8{pd}top{pd}sortby=acc{pd}reverse`` - #5-8 lowest acc top-100 scores on beatmap 495651
